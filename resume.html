Christina Truong

Tech Educator and Content Creator

Profile

Christina is a passionate tech educator and content creator with a mission to make technology accessible and engaging for all. With over 10 years of experience in the tech industry, she specializes in breaking down complex concepts into easy-to-understand tutorials and resources. From coding and software development to the latest in AI, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies, Christina creates content that empowers learners at all levels to expand their skills and stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

In addition to content creation, Christina speaks at tech conferences and workshops, advocating for inclusive, hands-on learning in STEM. Her goal is to inspire the next generation of innovators to embrace the power of technology for positive change.
